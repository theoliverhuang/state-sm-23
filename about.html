    About SPRING

    The SPRING Group is a youth-led think tank dedicated to adding young voices to public policy research, development, and advocacy. The group is organized on a chapter system with local chapters that focus on local public policy and a national level, focusing on national research. In the past, we’ve submitted papers to the UN, Center for AI and Digital Policy (CAIDP), and the European Commission. We also collaborate with governments and policymakers, such as the United Kingdom's Minister of Science & Georgia Senator Ossoff and Warnock.

    About SPRING Program on Health and Society

    SPRING’s Health and Society focuses on adding youth voices to public health and health policy. We focus on a variety of health issues, including digital wellness, health equity, and public health prevention.
